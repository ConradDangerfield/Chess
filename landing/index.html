// about

Somewhere between artist, builder,
and chaos gremlin.

Conrad Dangerfield lives at the intersection of music, visuals, code, game design, AI tinkering, and old-school web energy. The kind of person who builds a chess engine and then wonders if it could run inside a Discord voice channel. The kind who publishes half-finished audio sketches at 2 AM because they sounded cool in headphones.


If you knew the old version of this domain years ago — this is a reboot. Same name, newer toys, stronger weirdness.
